TRANSLATOR PARKING

Elevator slide type - Non-pallet type A translator is elevating and running at the same time to allow vehicles for entry and exit on parking space such as automated warehouse. You can control the number and moving lines of the vehicles by arranging the number and location of entrance and exit easily

Features

• Up to 100 parking space per lift

• Possible to increase parking space by installation of additional lifts

• Shortening of time by entry and exit simultaneously (average 1 minute)

• It can be used semipermanently by installing concrete on parking lot and be easy for maintenance.

• Construction period can be shortened by forming steel frame on it

• Lower floor height is needed for parking lot, which result in maximizing utilization of the basement floor when planning new buildings.
