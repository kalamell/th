โครงการ "อาคารสำนักงาน T1"

The Teo Hong Silom Group’s beginnings date to almost 37 years in 1974. Founded by Mr.Phaisan Sirirat-usdorn, The company quickly earned a reputation for astute business practices based on financial strength and the industry expertise of a professional management team with many years of business experience. The company initially concentrated on providing electrical products  primarily for use in government and private sector projects. However, being a progressive business, it soon diversified into new areas of commerce and within 10 years the company had interests in the building construction and property industries. In addition, it had begun to form joint ventures with a number of overseas companies.
